Major Achievements

The image above is Stephen Curry at the 2024 Paris Olympics. He made a memorable Olympic debut at the Paris 2024 Olympics, playing a pivotal role in leading Team USA to its fifth consecutive gold medal in men's basketball. Despite initial shooting struggles during the group stages, Curry’s performance peaked during the knockout rounds, showcasing his resilience and exceptional skill.

In the semifinal against Serbia, Team USA faced a unexpected 17-point deficit. Curry delivered a historic performance, scoring 36 points (including nine three-pointers) and grabbing eight rebounds. His efforts spearheaded a remarkable comeback, securing a 95–91 victory and setting a national record for the most points in an Olympic knockout game.

The momentum continued into the final against France. Curry tallied 24 points, all from three-point range, with a remarkable shooting accuracy of 8-of-12 from beyond the arc. In the closing minutes, he sank FOUR critical three-pointers, thwarting France's rally and sealing a 98–87 win for the United States. His iconic "Night Night" celebration after these decisive shots became a highlight of the tournament.

Throughout the tournament, Curry averaged 14.8 points per game, leading Team USA in scoring. His stellar performances earned him a spot on the Olympics All-Star Five team, placing him alongside legends like LeBron James. This achievement also positioned Curry among the elite group of players who have won multiple NBA championships, league MVP awards, a Finals MVP, and an Olympic gold medal.

Curry’s contributions were instrumental in maintaining Team USA’s dominance in international basketball, and his Olympic journey added a significant chapter to his illustrious career.

Stephen Curry has had an illustrious career with several notable achievements:

  • 4× NBA Champion (2015, 2017, 2018, 2022)
  • 2× NBA MVP (2015, 2016 - first unanimous MVP in history)
  • 9× NBA All-Star
  • NBA Three-Point Record Holder
  • Member of the 50-40-90 Club

His ability to shoot from long range has changed the way basketball is played at all levels. Curry's leadership and commitment to excellence continue to inspire future generations.
